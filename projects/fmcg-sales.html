FMCG Daily Sales Analysis

Exploratory Data Analysis on 190K+ retail sales records to uncover revenue drivers, promotion impact, and operational efficiency insights.

Python Pandas NumPy Matplotlib Seaborn

Project Overview

This project analyzes 190,000+ daily FMCG sales records including product, region, pricing, promotion, and stock data to identify revenue drivers and operational performance patterns.

Objective

Identify sales trends, product performance drivers, and measure the impact of promotions, pricing strategies, and stock availability.

Data Engineering

Handled missing values, removed duplicates, corrected data types, engineered new features such as revenue and delivery efficiency, and extracted time-based attributes to enhance analysis quality.

Key Insights

  • Sales distribution is right-skewed, indicating high-volume outliers.
  • Promotions impacted ~15% of sales but showed strong performance variation.
  • Revenue trends reveal seasonal fluctuations across regions.
  • Stock and delivery efficiency strongly influence fulfillment performance.
